### I. Scammer Report

To report an individual as a scammer, you must provide clear and complete evidence.

* **Required Content:** Clearly and accurately state the fraudulent actions of the person being reported.
* **Solid Evidence (MANDATORY):**
    * Videos, images.
    * **Trade history from backpack.tf (HIGHLY RECOMMENDED).**

### II. Joining the Highly Trusted Trader List

Applicants must meet the following criteria:

* **Verified statistics from backpack.tf** with **over 200 completed trades.**
* **Minimum Requirement:** Have **more than 20 positive ratings.**

> **NOTE:** While your profile will pass the basic requirements if met, **any suspicious activity will result in an automatic rejection.**

### III. Appeal (Appeal Against Scammer Listing)

If your account is listed as a Scammer, you have the right to submit an appeal.

* **Content:** Clearly explain your reasons and **get straight to the point.**
* **Evidence:** Provide evidence to support your case.
* **Verification:** Trade statistics from **backpack.tf will be used for verification.**

## üèÜ TRUSTED Point System (For HIGHLY TRUSTED TRADERS)

Every "Highly Trusted Trader" starts with **3/3 TRUSTED Points.**

* **Warning (2/3 Points):** If you lose 1 point, you will be downgraded to the **"IMPERSONATION RISK"** category.
* **Revocation (0/3 Points):** If you lose all points (0/3 points remaining), you will be **removed from the "HIGHLY TRUSTED TRADER" list** and placed into **"BLACKLISTED SCAMMER!"**

### 1. Point Restoration (Applies to "IMPERSONATION RISK")

Members in the **"IMPERSONATION RISK" (2/3 Points)** status can restore their points and return to **"HIGHLY TRUSTED TRADER"** status through one of the following methods:

* **Appeal:** Submit an appeal along with evidence proving your integrity.
* **Complete Transactions:** Complete **20 large, undisputed transactions**, with a value of **15 to 30 keys or more.**
* **NOTE:** Circular trades (using known or alternate (alt) accounts for trading) will be **rejected for verification.**

### 2. Serious Offenses

* If you are reported for scamming in a **large transaction (valued at 15 to 30 keys or more)**, you will be **immediately moved to "BLACKLISTED SCAMMER!"** and **cannot appeal.**
